Feature Focus

Smart Posture Tracking

Our cushion is embedded with a matrix of highly sensitive pressure sensors. These sensors create a detailed map of your sitting patterns, tracking the subtle shifts in weight across your glutes and thighs. This helps the device understand your sitting habits in real-time.

Feature Focus

Advanced Analysis

The raw pressure data is processed by our proprietary machine learning algorithm. It intelligently identifies patterns consistent with poor posture, such as slouching, leaning, or crossing your legs for extended periods. It learns your unique habits to provide feedback that is personalized to meet your goals.

Feature Focus

Timely Alerts

When the device detects a posture that could lead to strain, it sends a subtle notification via our companion app. These alerts are designed to be a mindful nudge, not an annoying interruption, empowering you to make a conscious correction before discomfort sets in.

Feature Focus

Seamless Comfort

Our technology adapts to you, and not the other way around. The Postura cushion is crafted from premium, breathable foam that provides support without altering the ergonomics of your favorite chair. It's sleek, discreet design can fit on any chair, ensuring comfort and relief all day long.
